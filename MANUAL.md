# BIN NIGHT — Player's Manual

*A tag-team trash heist for Playdate. One street. One night. Three mouths to feed.*

## The story

It's collection night in the suburbs, and at dawn the garbage truck comes
down the street eating the bins one house at a time. Trash Panda, Bin
Chicken and Alley Cat have until then to crack, ferry and sprint the
night's loot back to their alley cache. Meet the calorie quota and the
gang feasts — and moves on to a meaner street. Miss it, and the gang goes
hungry.

## Controls

| Input | Action |
| --- | --- |
| D-pad | Move (8-way) |
| A | Context action: rummage / flip lid / beak-plunge / pick up / drop |
| A (hold) | Alley Cat sprints; Bin Chicken flies (stamina-limited) |
| B (tap) | Character special: Panda BIN-TIP / Chicken SQUAWK / Cat POUNCE |
| B (hold ~⅓s) | Swap to the next character (Panda → Chicken → Cat) |
| Crank | Trash Panda pries latched bins open |

## How to play

You control ONE animal at a time; the other two are parked but keep
working (see "Parked jobs"). Loot only counts once it reaches the **alley
cache** at the far left of the street — walk a carried item past the
alley line, or drop it there; anything on the ground inside the alley
banks itself. A prompt over your head always names the A-button verb for
the bin you're standing at.

The clock runs 10PM to 5AM (about two and a half minutes). At 5AM the
truck arrives at the far right and works toward the cache, pausing at
each house to claw its curbside bins — everything still inside is gone
forever. The night ends when the truck leaves the street.

### The trio — every character is a verb the others lack

**TRASH PANDA — the Hands.** Slow, but lifts anything, including the
heavies (turkey, melon half, whole cake) and THE LASAGNA — he just drags
them at a stagger. Only he can **crank-pry** latched bins: wind the crank
a full turn, and brace for two scripted SLIPs that spin part of your
progress back. His **B special TIPS a whole bin over**, spilling all its
loot on the ground at once — fast, and very, very loud. He can't jump or
fly; at a fence he wriggles under (1.2s, helpless).

**BIN CHICKEN — the Reach.** Medium speed; hold A to fly over fences,
sprinklers and dogs (short stamina bar, recharges on the ground; each
flap makes a little noise). Her **beak-plunge** (A at a closed lidded
bin) steals small items through the lid gap almost silently — no opening
required — and she's the only one who can reach into the deep dumpster,
or eat the **worms** in compost bins (3 per bin, +30 cal each, banked
instantly). Her **B special is the SQUAWK**: a huge decoy noise — dogs
AND angry homeowners chase HER for the next 3 seconds while she flaps
away. She carries small and medium items (flying with a medium drains
stamina double-fast). Dogs can't bite her while she's airborne.

**ALLEY CAT — the Legs.** Fastest, and hold A to sprint. Hops any fence
without slowing. Her **B special is the POUNCE**: kills rats (+15 cal
"PROTEIN") and scares the possum off your loot. The catch: she carries
only small items, dogs aggro on her from more than double the distance,
and she flat refuses to enter a running sprinkler — she bounces off,
hissing.

### Parked jobs — the crew keeps working

- **Parked Panda** slowly drags whatever heavy item he's holding toward
  the cache. Leave him hauling the turkey and go do something else.
- **Parked Chicken** pecks at any open or tipped bin beside her, piling
  its loot on the ground every couple of seconds for someone to run home.
- **Parked Cat** guards: she auto-pounces any rat within reach and
  scares the possum. Park her at the cache or near your loot pile.
- All parked animals shuffle out of an awake dog's reach on their own.

### Bins

| Bin | Who opens it | What's inside |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Open-top | Anyone (A: rummage) | 3 small items |
| Lidded | Panda/Cat flip the lid (noisy) — Chicken beak-gaps smalls silently | 1 medium + 2 smalls |
| Latched | PANDA CRANK ONLY | a heavy (or THE LASAGNA) + a medium |
| Dumpster | Chicken plunges; Panda rummages slowly; Cat: too deep | 2 mediums + 3 smalls, sometimes THE LASAGNA |
| Compost dome | Chicken only | 3 worms, +30 cal each, banked on the spot |
| Recycling | Anyone — but it's EMPTY | Pure noise. The loudest sound in the game. A trap — or a deliberate decoy |

Panda's bin-tip works on anything except dumpsters and compost, and
spills the entire contents at once.

### Loot classes

| Class | Carriers | Examples | Calories |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Small | everyone | chip packet, sausage, chicken wing, fish bones, half sanga | 12–25 |
| Medium | Panda, Chicken | pizza box, noodle box, bread loaf | 40–55 |

## Scoring

- **TEAMWORK x1.5** — any item handled by two or more different animals
  between bin and cache. The intended play: Panda cracks, Chicken
  ferries, Cat runs it home.
- **DAREDEVIL x1.5** — loot pulled from a bin while the truck is right
  on top of it (working that very house, within claw range).
- **SOGGY x0.75** — loot that sat in a sprinkler burst.
- **Stealth bonus** — +50 at dawn for every house that never woke up.
- **Worms** +30 each (Chicken, compost). **Rats** +15 each (Cat).
- Quota is 450 calories on street 1 and climbs +200 per street. Your
  **career haul** (all streets this run) is the high score, saved to the
  device.

Multipliers stack: a daredevil teamwork item pays x2.25.

## Hazards

- **Noisy houses.** Every action rings a noise circle — beak plunge is a
  whisper, lid flips are a clunk, tips and squawks are a racket, and
  recycling clatter is the worst. Nearby houses get grumpier with each
  ring; past the threshold the porch light snaps on (that house's
  stealth bonus is gone) and its cone sweeps the yard. Move inside a lit
  cone for a moment too long and the homeowner storms out: he chases the
  animal he saw, and if he catches anyone carrying, the loot is
  **CONFISCATED** — tossed back into a random surviving bin. **Stand
  perfectly still in the light and you're invisible** — the
  raccoon-statue trick. He gives up and stomps inside after ~6 seconds.
- **Dogs.** Chained in some front yards, asleep until something loud
  (a lid flip or worse) goes off nearby. They chase anyone in range —
  the Cat from twice as far — and their barking wakes the house AND
  carries down the street. A bite means dropped, scattered loot and a
  stun. They can't cross the fence line, so footpath walkers are safe;
  they doze back off after a few quiet seconds.
- **Sprinklers.** Tick-tick-tick warning, then a 3-second burst. Ground
  loot caught in the spray goes SOGGY (-25%). Cat refuses to cross,
  Panda wades at half speed, Chicken flies over.
- **Rats.** If small loot lies near a storm drain, rats come out for it
  and drag it down the drain — gone. Anyone can loom close to scatter
  them; only the Cat's pounce (or her parked guard duty) kills them.
- **The Possum.** Shows up mid-night, waddles to the best loose item on
  the street and hauls it up a tree — gone. The Cat scares it just by
  getting near; the others have to practically touch it.
- **Night cars.** Headlights sweep the road at random until dawn. Get
  caught and you're cartoon-FLATTENED: stunned, loot scattered.
- **THE TRUCK.** At 5AM: air brakes, reverse beeper, headlights. It
  works right-to-left, pausing ~3.5 seconds per house while the claw
  grabs every curbside bin — contents lost, dots gone from the minimap.
  It skips houses with nothing left at the curb and shoves anyone
  standing in the road. Compost domes sit up in the yard, so the truck
  never gets those.

## The HUD

Top: banked calories vs quota, the clock, and a street minimap (bin
dots, cache flag, truck block, your blinking position). Bottom-left:
three crew chips — the filled one is active, a dot above means carrying,
a flashing outline means that parked character is in trouble RIGHT NOW.
Swap and deal with it.

## Streets

Street 1, Quiet Crescent, is handcrafted and gentle — two dogs, one
sprinkler, the lasagna in the house-2 dumpster. Every street after is
generated meaner: more latched bins, more dogs, more sprinklers,
grumpier houses, +200 quota. How deep can you take the gang?

## Tips

1. **Run the relay.** Solo-hauling everything home wastes the x1.5.
   Panda tips a bin, swap away — parked Chicken piles loot — Cat sprints
   the pieces home. Two paws/wings/claws on an item is free money.
2. **Compost is free, safe calories.** 90 per dome, banked instantly, no
   carrying, barely any noise — and the truck can't take it. Sweep them
   early with the Chicken.
3. **Statue up.** Light snaps on while you're mid-yard? Freeze. The
   "seen" timer drains while you're still — wait out the light or the
   owner's patience instead of sprinting and getting caught.
4. **The squawk is a rescue tool.** Dog cornering your loaded Panda?
   Swap to the Chicken, squawk, and every dog and homeowner nearby
   forgets him for 3 seconds. Then fly away — they can't bite her in
   the air.
5. **Don't leave smalls lying near storm drains** — that's where rats
   spawn, and they only come when there's food out. Either run drops
   home promptly or park the Cat over the pile.
6. **Recycling bins hold nothing.** Rummaging one is the loudest noise
   in the game. Never do it near your work zone — do it deliberately at
   a house you've already woken, or to pull dogs off a teammate.
7. **Daredevil the truck.** The claw takes 3.5 seconds per house — long
   enough to snatch one more item at x1.5 from the very bin it's
   grabbing. The Cat's sprint makes it nearly safe.
8. **Drop it at the line.** Anything dropped on the ground inside the
   alley banks itself — you don't have to walk all the way in. And a
   heavy item left on Panda when you swap away comes home on its own.
