# Bin Night — itch.io page copy

**Tagline:** Panda pries, Chicken plunges, Cat sprints — loot the street before dawn.

## Description

It's collection night in the suburbs, and the garbage truck arrives at
dawn to eat the street bin by bin. Trash Panda, Bin Chicken and Alley Cat
have one night to crack, ferry and sprint the good stuff back to their
alley cache — and no one of them can do it alone. Panda's crank-pry opens
the latched bins and hauls the turkey; the Chicken's absurd beak steals
through closed lids and flies loot over fences; the Cat runs the last leg
and keeps the rats off the pile.

You play one animal at a time — the other two keep working while parked —
and every item handled by two or more of the crew banks at TEAMWORK x1.5.
Around you, the suburb fights back: grumpy homeowners and porch lights,
chained dogs, sprinklers, rats in the storm drains, a kleptomaniac possum,
and finally the truck itself — which you can rob out from under the claw
for a DAREDEVIL bonus. Meet the calorie quota and the gang feasts and
moves to a meaner street; miss it and the gang goes hungry.

## Features

- Three playable animals, each a verb the others lack: PRY, FLY, SPRINT
- Tag-team play: parked characters haul, pile and guard on their own
- TEAMWORK, DAREDEVIL, stealth and soggy modifiers — routing IS the game
- Crank-powered bin prying, complete with heartbreaking slips
- A living night: dogs, sprinklers, rats, the possum, night cars, and one
  very hungry garbage truck
- Six bin types, THE LASAGNA (400 cal, one per street), endless
  generated streets after the handcrafted first night
- All-synth suburban soundscape — the ibis SQUAWK is a gameplay mechanic
- 1-bit night rendering: light cones, porch glow and dithered dawn

## Controls

D-pad moves. A is the context action (rummage / flip / plunge / grab);
hold A to sprint (Cat) or fly (Chicken). B taps the character special —
bin-tip, squawk decoy, pounce — and holding B swaps animals. The crank
pries latched bins (Panda only).
